About Olin College

The Franklin W. Olin College of Engineering is located in Needham, MA, and aims to educate engineers differently. Courses at Olin encourage collaboration and teamwork over competition, and most courses have a large project component that frequently takes the place of quizzes or exams. Olin is also notable in that every student at Olin receives at least a half-tuition scholarship.
