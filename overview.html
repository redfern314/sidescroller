Project Overview

Principles of Engineering (PoE)

PoE is essentially Olin's intro to mechatronics class and is required for all majors. Three Arduino-based labs during the first half of the semester teach students about designing robust cross-discipline systems. The second half of the semester is composed of a final team project that must have a significant mechanical, electrical, and software component.
